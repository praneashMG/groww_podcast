Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about listening, creating, and growing with GrowwPodcast.

General Questions

What is GrowwPodcast?

GrowwPodcast is a global platform for discovering, listening to, and hosting podcasts. We connect creators with listeners through high-quality audio content, community features, and powerful growth tools.

Is GrowwPodcast free to use?

Yes! Listeners can access thousands of episodes for free. We also offer a Premium subscription for ad-free listening and exclusive content. For creators, we have a free starter plan and paid tiers for advanced analytics and monetization.

For Creators

How do I upload my podcast?

Simply create an account, go to your Dashboard, and click "New Episode." You can upload MP3 or WAV files, add show notes, and publish instantly or schedule for later. We also support RSS import from other hosts.

Can I monetize my show?

Absolutely. We offer dynamic ad insertion, listener support (tips), and premium subscriptions. Monetization features are available on our "Professional" and "Network" plans.

What equipment do I need?

You can start with just a smartphone! However, for better quality, we recommend a USB microphone (like the Blue Yeti or Samson Q2U) and a pair of headphones. Check out our blog for detailed equipment guides.

Technical Support

Can I listen offline?

Yes, our mobile app allows you to download episodes for offline listening. Just tap the download icon next to any episode to save it to your library.

What audio formats do you support?

We accept MP3, M4A, and WAV files. We recommend uploading MP3s at 128kbps or higher for the best balance of quality and file size.
