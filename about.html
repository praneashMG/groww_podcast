Our Story

Amplifying Voices,
Inspiring Change.

Founded in 2015, GrowwPodcast started with a simple microphone and a mission: to democratize knowledge. Today, we are a global community connecting curious minds with industry leaders, innovators, and storytellers.

Our Vision

"To empower individuals worldwide by providing free, accessible, and high-quality audio content that educates, entertains, and elevates the human experience."

Our Mission

"To build a borderless world where knowledge flows freely, every voice finds a stage, and audio becomes the catalyst for global understanding."

Accessibility

Knowledge should have no paywalls. We are committed to keeping 90% of our content free for everyone.

Diversity

We actively seek voices from underrepresented communities to ensure a rich tapestry of perspectives.

Quality

We don't just record; we craft. Every episode is researched, produced, and edited to the highest standards.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every story we tell.

Empathy First

We listen to understand, not just to reply. Every guest story is treated with dignity.

Curiosity

We never stop asking "Why?". We dig deeper to find the hidden gems in every topic.

Integrity

We fact-check, verify, and deliver truth. Trust is our most valuable currency.

Innovation

We push the boundaries of audio storytelling, embracing new formats and tech.
